Palakkad Must Visit Food Spots

Spot 1: NMR Biriyani

NMR Restaurant

Located inside Palakkad Town, N.M.R Biriyani serves a peculiar tasting biriyani, which is a mix between Tamil Nadu and Kerala style Biriyanies. Tried their chicken, beef biriyani and chilly beef fry. Biriyani was on the spicy side. And the price was also less compared to other hotels which serve worse biriyanis. If you are coming to Palakkad, it's a must-visit restaurant.
